ऊनको स्वीटर Unko Sweater

Unko Sweater is a Nepali romantic social drama that explores love across cultural and caste boundaries. The story follows Dharanidhar, a young man from a traditional Brahmin family, who falls in love with Phool, a free-spirited girl from the Gurung community. Their relationship faces resistance from family expectations and deep-rooted social norms, portraying themes of identity, acceptance, and social change.
