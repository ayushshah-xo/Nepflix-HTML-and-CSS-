Frequently Asked Questions

Nepflix is a Nepali streaming platform dedicated to Nepali cinema and original content. Created to celebrate and promote Nepali film culture, it offers a curated library of the best movies from Nepal — including top hits like Pashupati Prasad, Loot, Bulbul, and Kagbeni.

Nepflix is free for demonstration and learning projects. In a full release, flexible subscription plans would be available starting from affordable monthly rates to suit every budget.

You can watch Nepflix on Smart TVs, PlayStation, Xbox, Chromecast, Apple TV, Blu-ray players, laptops, tablets, and mobile devices — anywhere with an internet connection.

You can cancel anytime from your account settings — no fees, no hassle. Your membership continues until the end of the current billing period with no extra charges.

Nepflix offers an extensive library including feature films, comedies, dramas, action movies, romantic films, and classic Nepali cinema — with top-rated titles such as Pashupati Prasad, Loot, Kagbeni, Bulbul, Seto Surya, Chhakka Panja, and many more.
